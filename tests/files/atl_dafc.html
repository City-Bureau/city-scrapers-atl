The Development Authority of Fulton County (DAFC) is mandated by law to hold a monthly meeting. The DAFC is scheduled to meet on the 4th Tuesday of every month at:

Fulton County Government Administration Building 141 Pryor Street, SW 2nd Floor Conference Room, Suite 2052, Peachtree Level Atlanta, GA 30303

COVID-19:

Due to concerns surrounding the spread of COVID-19 in Fulton County and nearby communities, President Biden’s Continuation of the National Emergency Concerning Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) Pandemic, and DAFC’s finding that COVID-19 conditions continue to exist in Fulton County, DAFC will be holding certain public meetings via teleconference and videoconference in accordance with the Open Meetings Act (See O.C.G.A. Section 50-14-1 (g)).

While certain public meetings may be physically conducted in DAFC’s conference room at 141 Pryor Street SW, Suite 2052 (Peachtree Level), Atlanta, Georgia 30303, DAFC will be implementing appropriate social distancing per CDC guidelines. In the event a quorum of board members are unable to attend such in-person meetings in person due to COVID-19 precautions and related conditions not conducive to in-person appearance, the meeting will be held with a quorum of members participating by videoconference/teleconference in compliance with the emergency provisions of the Open Meetings Act.

We invite the public to participate in all public meetings via a Zoom webinar, which can be accessed by using the dial-in instructions provided at the top of each Preliminary Agenda for the applicable meeting. Please note that the dial-in instructions are unique for each meeting, so please refer to the Preliminary Agenda for the correct meeting date to ensure successful participation.

For questions regarding meetings, contact:
Doris M. Coleman
Doris.Coleman@fultoncountyga.gov
